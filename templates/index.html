About Us

Welcome to Health Lab

Health Lab is a website or service that provides its users the ability to schedule appointments with doctors, allowing patients to connect with doctors without a physical presence, where doctors can offer suggestions and prescriptions. Furthermore, Health Lab allows patients to share their health progress with doctors and facilitates telemedicine features through video calls.

Health Lab aims to make healthcare more accessible and convenient for patients by leveraging technology.By offering telemedicine features, Health Lab enables patients to receive medical care from the comfort of their own homes.
